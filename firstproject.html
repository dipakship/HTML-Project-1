Types of Galaxies

There are 3 main categories of galaxies: spiral, elliptical, and irregular.

  1. Spiral Galaxies

    • Known to be the first type of galaxies
    • Charcterized by their "spiral" shape
    • ex. Milky Way

  2. Elliptical Galaxies

    • Named after their oblong shape and no overall structure
    • Some may be more spherical while others are more oval shaped
    • ex. M87

  3. Irregular Galaxies

    • Any galaxy that is not characterized as a spiral or elliptical galaxy, as the name suggests
    • Tend to be small, dwarf galaxies
    • Usually lack any general shape
    • ex. Small and Large Magellanic clouds
